# Company Overview

## Company Name
Coding Temple

## Mission
Launch tech careers with job-ready bootcamps. We help career changers, graduates, and working professionals break into tech through intensive, hands-on training with lifetime career support.

## Stage
Growth

## Industry
EdTech / Coding Bootcamps

## Business Model
Tuition-based with multiple financing options:
- Upfront payment
- Month-to-month installments
- Deferred tuition
- Loan financing
- Employer-sponsored
- Tuition-free (select programs/scholarships)

Pricing ranges from ~$5,000 to $13,995 depending on program and format.

## Key Stats
- 97% job placement rate within 9 months of graduation (claimed)
- Alumni hired at Google, Amazon, Accenture, and others
- 4.88/5 stars on SwitchUp, 9.5/10 on BootcampRankings
- Nationwide availability (online)
